About the project

It is a store that sells star light projector. It projects a colourful starry sky onto the walls and ceiling, great for bedroom, living room...etc. They also sell other accessories along with the product.

The website was planned to be used more as a support to their existing sales on Instagram. Thus, the website should be easy to maintain and changed according to the Instagram content that they are currently handling.

Galaxy Box is doing affiliate marketing with a product called LED Galaxy. However, the default Shopify themes does not allows to create affiliate pages to promote any affiliate product in the store. By using Shopify Liquid and custom front-end coding, I was able to create affiliate pages for any affiliate product they are planning the market in their store. Now the Galaxy Box can promote affiliate marketing in their own store. They could even add any additional affiliate products in the future without the need of a developer afterwards.
