About the project

Two Clothes is a fashon wear brand. It holds a huge collection of clothes with wide ranges of variants.

I helped them to fully implement their products and collections making a full store setup. We decided together what kind of logo would fits with their website.

At the beginning we choosed a premium theme adapted to their kind of products.

How I helped to improve customer's visual experience

For this project I was asked to highlight the new arrivals on the Home page. To do this, I used the full power of the theme we had decided on using the special feature "shop to look".

As you can see on the image below, It basically shows an image displaying all the different newly arrived products. Interested customer would simply click on the specific product that is displayed on the image. When clicked, it will give more information about that product and they can proceed to the checkout directly for the product.
